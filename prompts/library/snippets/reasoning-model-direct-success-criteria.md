# Reasoning model direct success criteria

<!-- promptkit:start -->
For reasoning models:
- Keep the prompt direct and specific.
- Do not request a full chain-of-thought transcript.
- Define success criteria and ask the model to keep working until the answer satisfies them.
- Ask for a concise rationale and verification summary, not hidden scratch work.
