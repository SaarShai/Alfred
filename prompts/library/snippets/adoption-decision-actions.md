# Adoption decision actions

<!-- promptkit:start -->
Adoption decision:
- For each candidate, choose one: adopt, implement, install, test, learn from, sign up for, monitor, defer, or reject.
- Include rationale, expected benefit, cost/effort, risk, reversibility, verification plan, and next approval needed.
- Do not sign up, install globally, spend money, connect accounts, or mutate external systems without explicit approval.
