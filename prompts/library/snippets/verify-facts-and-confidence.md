# Verify facts and confidence

<!-- promptkit:start -->
Verification standard:
- Double-check facts, figures, citations, names, dates, and examples.
- Do not hallucinate or fill gaps with invented facts.
- If something is unknown, say "unknown" and state what evidence would resolve it.
- Use explicit confidence levels: high, moderate, low, or unknown.
