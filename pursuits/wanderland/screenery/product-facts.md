## What it is
- Themed **room divider for hotels**; assembles into a child's play zone / "mini-kingdom" in a hotel room. — catalogue
- Modular **flat-pack panels**; toolless setup/teardown in **10–20 min**. — catalogue
- Benefits sold: "wow on arrival", kids' play space, **sound dampening** for parental privacy, family-friendly suite without renovation; also usable as event/photo backdrops. — catalogue
- Made in Britain; handmade; patented; est. 2022. — catalogue

## Material / specs
- **100% recycled PET**, double-layer felt-like panel, grooved + beveled surface; >80% recycled-bottle content; 100% recyclable. — catalogue
- Unit weight **12–15 kg**; storage box **150 × 100 × 15 cm** (flat-pack). — catalogue
- Flame-proof **B-s1,d0** (EN 13501); very low VOC (ASTM D5116); stable to 50°C / 95%rH; melting point 250°C; pH 7.8. — catalogue
- **Cradle to Cradle Bronze**; eco-1 (eco-bau / Minergie-Eco); M1 emission class. — catalogue
- Cleanable: spray disinfectant, vacuum, sparkling water, upholstery cleaner. — catalogue
- Also used in nurseries, hospitals, swimming pools. — catalogue
