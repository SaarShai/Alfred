# Executive office hours

## When to Use

Use when the user is exploring an idea, choosing priorities, planning a week, evaluating whether something is worth doing, or asking for executive-level pushback.

## Modes

- Startup or product: test demand, status quo, target user, wedge, observation, and future fit.
- Builder or creative: optimize for delight, fastest shareable artifact, and what makes the user want to keep building.
- Personal operations: clarify stakes, deadlines, blockers, leverage, decision owner, and next action.

## Question Set

Ask only unanswered questions. Prefer one strong question at a time.

- What outcome matters, and by when?
- What is happening today instead?
- Who is the actual person affected?
- What is the narrowest useful version?
- What evidence or observation surprised you?
- What changes if we do nothing?
- What would make this feel obviously worth it?

## Output

Return:

- Reframed problem.
- Evidence and gaps.
- 2-3 approaches.
- Recommendation.
- Next concrete action.
- Open decisions.
