# Network map

How the principles route together. Hubs are entry points; attractors are the basins most principles fall into; edges are the load-bearing wiring.

## Hubs

Principles most others route back to (retrieval entry points).

- **[[Principles/now]]** (1) — "nothing is wrong NOW" / "Start now, no exceptions." Present-moment / anti-time-travel. *(user-confirmed)*
- **[[Principles/no-opinion]]** (7) + **[[Principles/less-thought]]** (8) — "all our negative feelings come from our thinking." Dissolve thinking. *(user-confirmed)*
- Connective spine: **reinterpretation** — happy = interpretations − expectations ([[Principles/happy-interpretations-minus-expectations]]) / [[Principles/context-over-content]]. Sits under both hubs and Acceptance. *(inferred)*
- Self-blame as a negative hub: *Alfred inference, user is NOT sure it's core — likely overstated by Alfred from loud confessional sections. Treat as a theme, not the center.*

## How the layers relate (user-stated)

**Principle** → **Mantra** (the shortest encoding of a principle, said often) → **Imagination** (a mental image/scene brought to mind to change mood or mindset).

**Section/cluster location and order carry no meaning** (user-stated) — do not infer adjacency, ordering, or "sits next to" relationships.

Principles are **fractal and interlinked**: a principle (e.g. a cluster name like Manifesting/Action/Stoicism) unpacks into sub-principles, and most principles cross-reference each other. This is why the wiki graph fits the archive — the structure is a network, not a list.

## Load-bearing cross-edges (not within one basin)

- H → G: inviting what you avoid (6/39) feeds seeking discomfort (32) and dissolving fear-of-fear (52).

- B → A: less thinking (8) is how you stay in NOW (1) — dissolve is the method, NOW is the destination.
