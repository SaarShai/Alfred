Pre-mortem hardened. Status lives on the Job Tracker row (factory updates a Stage dropdown), so there is no scattered status and no fire-and-forget email — the row is the single cross-job view. Alan commits Planned Start/Ship so delivery targets match real capacity. Saar opens the row (no dependency on a busy Alicia). Catalog reuse requires an exact match; variants force the bespoke path. Stock is treated as approximate — conservative reorder points + a monthly factory photo to reset truth. Protected ranges keep each actor's columns safe; the Confirmed Orders & Files-breakdown columns stay untouched. Roll out on one pilot order; Alicia & Alan co-sign their columns before standardising.
